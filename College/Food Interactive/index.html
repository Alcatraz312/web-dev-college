About Us

The Grand Food Court was established in 2010 with a simple vision — to bring the best cuisines from around the world to one convenient and comfortable location. Over the years, we have grown into one of the most popular dining destinations in the city, hosting over 20 restaurants and serving thousands of happy customers every day.

Whether you are looking for a quick bite during lunch, a family dinner, or a casual outing with friends, The Grand Food Court has something for everyone. Our food court is designed to be spacious, clean, and welcoming for all age groups.

Our Mission

To provide a diverse and high-quality dining experience in a clean, friendly, and affordable environment for everyone.

Why Choose Us?

20+ restaurants, ample seating, free Wi-Fi, kid-friendly zones, and a clean food-safe environment — all under one roof.

Our Story

Started by a group of food lovers in 2010, we have grown from 5 stalls to a full-scale food court with restaurants from 10+ countries.
